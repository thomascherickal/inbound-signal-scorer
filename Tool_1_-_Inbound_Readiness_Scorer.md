# Tool 1 — Inbound Readiness Scorer

**Week 1 · Available immediately on joining · From Chapter 1**

*"Before you can fix what's keeping you invisible to recruiters, you need to know exactly where your gaps are."*

---

### What It Does

The Inbound Readiness Scorer assesses your current position across the **8 core pillars of inbound recruitment** from Chapter 1 of RECRUITED. Each pillar is scored on a weighted 0–10 slider. The tool produces a **composite readiness score out of 100**, a visual breakdown by pillar, a letter grade with interpretation, and a **priority action list** ranked by impact and urgency — not generic advice, but actions specific to your actual lowest-scoring pillars.

### The 8 Pillars

1. **LinkedIn Presence & Posting Consistency** — weighted 20%. Measures posting frequency, profile completeness, Creator Mode status, engagement rate, and monthly profile view trajectory.
2. **Personal Brand Clarity & Niche Positioning** — weighted 18%. Assesses whether your niche is defined, consistent across platforms, and expressible in one sentence.
3. **Public Portfolio & Proof of Work** — weighted 15%. GitHub activity, published articles, case studies, pinned repositories, byline history.
4. **AI Tool Fluency** — weighted 12%. Depth of use across NotebookLM, Google Antigravity, Perplexity.ai, Google AI Studio, and GitHub Copilot.
5. **Content Output Frequency & Quality** — weighted 12%. Number of original pieces published in the last 30 days; originality vs. recycled content.
6. **Network Depth & Relationship Equity** — weighted 10%. Size and strength of inner circle (5–10 contacts), middle circle (20–30), and referral activation history.
7. **Hidden Market Access** — weighted 8%. Number of warm referral paths into target companies; contact depth at target employers.
8. **Thought Leadership Signal Strength** — weighted 5%. Whether you are known for a specific perspective, cited by others, or invited to contribute.

### Scoring Engine

Pillar scores are weighted and summed into a composite. Score bands map to five grades: **0–39 (F — Invisible)**, **40–54 (D — Passive)**, **55–69 (C — Emerging)**, **70–84 (B — Visible)**, **85–100 (A — Magnetic)**. Each grade triggers a specific interpretation: what your profile looks like to a recruiter right now, what the most common objection to hiring you would be, and what single change would move your score the most.

### Priority Action List

The tool generates a ranked list of **3–5 specific actions**, each tagged Critical / High / Medium, drawn from the pillar with the lowest weighted score. Actions are not generic ("post more content") — they are specific to the pillar, score band, and the frameworks in RECRUITED. A score of 2/10 on LinkedIn Presence generates different actions than a score of 6/10 on the same pillar.

### Additional Features

- **Printable PDF output** — one-page summary with score, pillar breakdown, and action list. Designed for printing and pinning.
- **Rescore button** — retake after implementing changes. Timestamps each session so you can track improvement over weeks.
- **Shareable score card** — generates a PNG image of your score suitable for sharing on LinkedIn as accountability content.
- **Progress tracker** — logs up to 12 scoring sessions locally, rendering a line graph of your composite score over time.

### Why It's the Best in Its Category

Every other "career readiness" assessment on the market is either (a) a generic quiz with no weighting or (b) a lead magnet that sends you a PDF and asks for your email. This tool is weighted, specific, actionable, re-takeable, timestamped, and printable — and it's free, with no email required.
